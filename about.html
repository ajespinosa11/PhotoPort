Aldrin Jasson Espinosa

In the bustling streets of Manila, a new passion was born in 2023—a love for photography. Armed with a camera, I embarked on a journey to capture the heartbeat of my city, one snapshot at a time. From the colorful chaos of Quiapo to the tranquil shores of Manila Bay, my lens became my window to the soul of Manila. Through each click, I sought to freeze moments that told stories—of laughter echoing through crowded alleys, of quiet moments by the bay, and of the resilience of its people. Though challenges arose, from navigating urban hardships to confronting poverty, my camera remained my steadfast companion. With every photo, I aimed not just to document, but to inspire change and highlight the beauty amidst the chaos. And so, my passion for photography continues to grow, fueled by the desire to capture Manila's diverse tapestry and share its untold stories with the world.
